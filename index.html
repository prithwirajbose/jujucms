Welcome to JujuCMS

JujuCMS is a Java & Struts 2 and Tiles based Content Management System by JujuApps. It supports Custom Templates, Custom Struts 2 Action and gives you full control over the code-base of the underlying Content Management System. Unlike OpenCMS or other complex Content Management Systems, JujuCMS is much simpler, flexible and customizable.
